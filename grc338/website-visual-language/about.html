About us

Birthed in the heart of southern California, The Early Worm. started as a food truck serving bagel sandwiches in the sunny Anaheim streets. Lots of early mornings moving egg crates to serve the locals for both their morning endeavors and afternoon happenings.

Word soon spread, and our little food truck quickly became a beloved morning ritual for many. As the lines grew longer, so did our dream of creating a space where people could slow down and savor the start of their day. That dream finally took root in our first brick-and-mortar café, a cozy spot filled with the same warmth and hustle that fueled us from the beginning.

Every recipe on our menu still carries a piece of those early mornings on the streets—simple, honest, and made with care. We’re proud to continue serving the community that lifted us up, one hearty breakfast at a time. And no matter how much we grow, The Early Worm will always stay true to its humble sunrise beginnings."
